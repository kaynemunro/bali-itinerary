Packing for July

Light clothing, reef-safe sunscreen, a hat, and one light layer for boats & air-con. Short showers are possible but rarely last. Pack seasickness pills — take them 30–60 min before each boat crossing & the snorkel day (they work preventively).

Boats & sea

July is dry season — calmest seas of the year. Mornings are smoothest; afternoons pick up. Crossings: Kai Koa (17th, 12:30pm) · Captain Yin Yang snorkel (19th, ~8:30am) · Rocky home (22nd, 11am).

The golden rule

Build traffic buffers everywhere — especially the Bali airport run on the 22nd. We've padded every transfer.

Island stay, sorted

17–20 at Morin Resort, then a short hop to Lembongan Beach Club for 20–22 — both in Jungutbatu.

Villa breakfast

Villa Zayya breakfast isn't automatic — if you want to eat in, you must pre-order with the villa (usually the day before). Breakfast is already included at Morin & Lembongan Beach Club. (Booking.com listing)
